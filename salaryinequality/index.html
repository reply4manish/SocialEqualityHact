About

Where do you get your information?

We base our estimates on information provided by the Statistiska Centralbyrån, sorted by over 700 types of jobs as well as gender and ethnicity. This is then measured against information provided by our users within the last three years, to come to a close approximation of your situation.

Should I use your site to sue my employers?

No. We aim to achieve as close as possible an approximation of the salary for people in your position, but can not make any guarantees for the conduct of your employers specifically. Instead, we suggest you use our information to better assess the value of your work, and then engage in an open discussion with your employer based on merit and experience.

How is my privacy warranted?

All supplied information is anonymized, and is linked only to your broad demographic data. Individual data is never displayed in the results, and information you provide is not traceable to individual organisations or employees.

I suspect I’m suffering from a wage gap for my sexual preference, religion or disability. Can I still use your tool?

Yes, you can - just select your situation from the fine tune menu. Note, however, that the Statistiska Centralbyrån does not provide info for these situations specifically, meaning our estimates will be broader and our info somewhat less accurate.
